Solar Outdoor Lamp Thing

What I used:

5V USB plug in bulb
4xAA Batteries
2x 5V Panels
Diode
LDR
Plastic Bottle Body
MT 3608 DC Voltage boost.


Plug in panels in parallel, batteries in series, positive of the panels to diode and LDR, then the positive of the batteries. Connect the positive of the batteries to VIN+ on the board
